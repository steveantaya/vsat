You bundle them in 50\'92s or 100\'92s for the post office. \ Visit your local post office and determine the best routes to target or you can use the EDDM website with an integrated map that highlights \ the specific routes and counts for you. https://eddm.usps.com/eddm/customer/routeSearch.action \ **Note: You do not need to drop to an entire zip code. \ With EDDM you are targeting the entire route in an area that makes sense for you to mail in.
